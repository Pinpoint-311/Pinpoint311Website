Features

Everything a modern municipality needs.
Nothing you don't.

UX
No-login submission
Residents submit in under 60 seconds with no account. Email magic links let them track status without passwords.
ML
109 language support
Google Translate powers full UI translation including service categories, status labels, and all notification emails in the resident's language.
PH
Multi-photo upload
Up to 3 high-res images with client-side compression. Photos feed directly into AI visual analysis.
TL
Status timeline
Clean visualization of the request journey: Received → In Progress → Resolved → Closed, with SMS and email updates.
MP
Public request map
Interactive map of all open and resolved requests, filterable by department, status, date range, and service type.
RT
Smart routing
Road-based routing rules automatically redirect state or county road reports to the right agency with custom messaging.
LF
Live feed
Auto-refreshes every 30 seconds with "NEW" badging. Split-pane view with independent scrolling for list and detail.
AI
AI insights panel
Safety flags, proximity to schools and hospitals, sentiment analysis, weather context, and similar nearby reports — all powered by Gemini 3.1 Flash-Lite.
HI
Human-in-the-loop priority
AI suggests a 1–10 urgency score. Staff must explicitly accept it — scores are never automatically saved, ensuring human accountability.
CO
Collaboration tools
Private internal comments for staff coordination. External updates visible to residents. Full immutable audit log of every action.
CA
AI analytics advisor
Conversational AI interface powered by Gemini 3.1 Flash-Lite. Ask natural language questions about your data — equity gaps, triage times, response patterns.
AH
Asset history
When a request is tied to a physical asset (e.g., Hydrant #404), automatically surfaces all past reports for that specific asset.
BR
Full white-label branding
Township logo, colors, and font settings automatically injected into every page, email, and SMS notification.
SR
Service routing rules
Route each service to internal departments, third-party contractors, or road-based jurisdiction splits — all without code.
GL
Custom GeoJSON layers
Upload GeoJSON to visualize township assets on the staff map — parks, storm drains, zoning districts, hydrants.
UP
One-click updates
Pull the latest code from GitHub and rebuild containers with a single button. Database backup and health check included automatically.
DR
Document retention
State-specific retention policies (TX, NJ, CA, etc.) with legal hold support and automatic Celery Beat archival.
LG
Legal pages
Fully customizable Privacy Policy, Terms of Service, and Accessibility Statement with sensible municipal defaults built in.
GF
Boundary enforcement
Point-in-polygon checks against uploaded GeoJSON boundaries. Requests dropped outside jurisdiction are auto-rejected.
AS
Asset selection
Residents click directly on infrastructure assets on the map and select the specific one their report relates to.
HS
Hotspot detection
PostGIS ST_ClusterDBSCAN automatically clusters requests to identify problem areas, like pothole clusters on specific roads.
MC
Marker clustering
Auto-groups nearby markers on the resident-facing map via Google Maps MarkerClusterer for clean visual density.
PX
Proximity analysis
AI checks if issues are near critical infrastructure — schools, hospitals, fire stations — using PostGIS with Nominatim fallback.
BD
User bias detection
Spatial statistics flag suspicious activity, like a single user submitting dozens of reports within a 10-meter radius.
Intelligence

AI that works
for your staff,
not around them.

Every AI action requires human approval. No score is ever auto-applied — creating a full audit trail of who decided what and when.

1
Visual analysis
Gemini 3.1 Flash-Lite "sees" uploaded photos to categorize hazards and provide richer context for staff triage.
2
Multilingual AI
When residents submit in non-English, AI provides a literal English translation first so staff can read every submission.
3
Conversational analytics advisor
Ask plain English questions: "Are there equity gaps in our response times?" Get data-driven answers with real numbers.
4
PII auto-redaction
Names, phones, and emails are automatically stripped from public logs before any AI or researcher ever sees the data.

Security & Compliance

Enterprise security.
Government-grade.

A0
Auth0 SSO
Zero-password authentication with MFA, passkeys, biometrics, and social login. No passwords stored anywhere in the system.
KM
Google Cloud KMS
All resident PII (email, phone, name, address) encrypted with AES-256-GCM via HSM-backed Google Cloud KMS.
AU
Immutable audit logs
Every lifecycle event — submission, assignment, comment, status change — is logged for OPRA/FOIA compliance and legal discovery.
SC
CI/CD security scanning
CodeQL static analysis, OWASP ZAP dynamic testing, and Trivy container scanning on every push. Dependabot for weekly dependency updates.
WC
WCAG 2.1 AA
Full keyboard navigation, 4.5:1 contrast ratio, and aria-labels on all interactive elements. Pa11y accessibility audits in CI.
DR
Data sovereignty
Fully self-hosted on your infrastructure. No phone-home to Pinpoint 311 servers. MIT license means you can fork and maintain indefinitely.
