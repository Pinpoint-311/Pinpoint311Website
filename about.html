Democratizing Municipal Infrastructure

Pinpoint 311 is a [501(c)(3) nonprofit](https://hackclub.com/fiscal-sponsorship/) fiscally sponsored by the [Hack Club Foundation](https://hackclub.com/). This status allows us to receive tax-deductible donations and focus entirely on our mission of public service.
