About Pinpoint 311

Built for communities.
Not for profit.

Pinpoint 311 is a free, open-source municipal reporting platform created so every community — regardless of budget — can access modern infrastructure tools.

Our Mission
Give every municipality world-class tools.

Free forever

No SaaS pricing. No tiers. The platform is MIT licensed and always will be.

Open source

Every line of code is public. Fork it, audit it, modify it.

AI-assisted

Gemini-powered triage and analysis — but always human-approved.

Equity focused

Built-in research datasets allow municipalities to study infrastructure equity.

Founder
Meet the person behind Pinpoint 311

Parth Gupta

Founder & Lead Developer

A West Windsor, NJ resident for 14 years and student at the Lawrenceville School, Parth built Pinpoint 311 after seeing how infrastructure issues in his community went unreported or got lost between voicemails and spreadsheets.

He wanted to prove that modern civic infrastructure tools don't need to cost six figures.
